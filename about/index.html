I am on a mission to make long stories short: passionate about speaking visual languages driven by conceptual thinking and curiosity. I believe that design is about courage and cross-platform, so my diverse background is at the intersection of research, product, management, art, code, design and urban planning. The goal is to spread holistic perspectives and a great experience, bring clarity and beauty, always have fun.

Plasticity implies internal density and resistance capability. We can mold something out of it, but not everything. It's as if there's an explosive charge inside, limiting compression. Flexibility, as endless adaptation to conditions, isn't my preference. Plasticity, as controlled adaptation, maintaining internal density, seems a richer and more sustaining metaphor.

My mission is to create digital experiences that not only align with current trends but also preserve the brand's essence. My services include brand strategy development, unique visual identity creation, website design, presentations, and packaging.

Every project begins with a deep study of the business to find the core unique idea. Approach is based on four principles: simplicity, functionality, partnership and uniqueness.

At the beginning of my journey I was often radical (like many beginners), now I do not set restrictions, I often work at the intersection of disciplines1–3, but my design I want to describe with the following quote:

«It is easy to think that beauty resides in the realm of creativity. Yet beauty hardly «appears» from nowhere. Recently, I have come to believe that we «discover» it through the cleaning and polishing we do to preserve things as they are. [...] The beauty of a temple garden rests not in the splendid features that were created by a talented designer; rather, its beauty is uncovered through the continual process of cleaning.»

― Kenya Hara, White
