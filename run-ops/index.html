Visual Identity for RUN-OPS, everyday business solutions company, that helps brands to professionally navigate finances in a very buddy and flexible format. The brand provides an opportunity for any business, regardless of its scale, to receive expert consultation and afford the level of advice that was previously only available to large companies. This was captured on the brand identity through a Buddy line concept that symbolizes moving towards solving the problem in a friendly TOV. It is a living element that takes different forms.

Purpose: The visual identity for RUN-OPS aims to blend professionalism with approachability, providing expert financial guidance in a friendly format. Challenge: Balancing professionalism and friendliness while reflecting the brand's non-conformist spirit and energetic approach. Reflect: non-conformism, very professional, freedom, buddy, energetical, passionate. The design captures RUN-OPS' commitment to transparency, accuracy, and innovation, offering tailored solutions with a playful touch. Mission: Empower businesses of all sizes with confident, clear financial guidance in a buddy-like format. Vision: Redesign financial management with transparency, freedom, and innovation, becoming the trusted partner for everyday business success. Essence: Professionalism meets camaraderie, characterized by transparency, accuracy, and passion for supporting business growth.

The Van de Graaf Canon, or Canon grid, adapts to the proportions of its medium, providing a structured foundation for design. While it emphasizes starting from this grid, it also encourages creative deviation when necessary for impactful design solutions. The grid remains invisible in the final layouts. But all the resulting designs look very well-adjusted, but not monotonous.

The result of the work yielded a design that exudes professionalism and clarity. However, it's crucial to remember that the core concept revolves around the Buddy Line, which allows for playful illustrations and forms. Similar to margin notes, these elements inject liveliness and individuality into the design, while maintaining its structure and professionalism.
