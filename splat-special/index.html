Work

Splat Special

Role
Product & Packaging Design
Discipline
Beauty and care

Introducing Splat Special: a revolutionary toothpaste experience that transcends norms. It's about caring for your smile differently. Concepts: Splat Special offers a unique approach to toothpaste with its meticulously crafted textures, colors, and aromas for each SKU. Transparent tubes showcase the unreal textures of the paste, while metallic accents add a futuristic touch. This departure from conventional packaging emphasizes the product itself as the hero, inviting consumers to explore a new dimension in oral care. Drawing inspiration from the world of perfumery, Special is positioned as a selective toothpaste brand. Featuring a stable metallic pump, reminiscent of something from another planet, it offers refill options with vibrant prints for customization. This concept not only embodies technological advancement but also evokes a sense of hedonism and pleasure, making every aspect of the oral care routine a luxurious experience.
